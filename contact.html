Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of car care services including basic wash, premium wash, interior detailing, full detailing, ceramic coating, and paint correction. We also provide additional services like engine bay cleaning and headlight restoration.

Do you provide mobile services?

Yes, we offer mobile services where we come to your location. This is perfect for busy professionals or those who prefer the convenience of having their car serviced at home or work.

How long does a typical service take?

Service duration varies: Basic wash (1-2 hours), Premium wash (2-3 hours), Interior detail (3-4 hours), Full detail (4-6 hours), Ceramic coating (1-2 days), Paint correction (1-3 days depending on condition).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion of the service.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our services. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make it right at no additional cost.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website, by calling us directly, or by sending us a message. We'll confirm your booking and provide all necessary details.
